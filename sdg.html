Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

  • 6.1.1: Safe drinking water services
  • 6.2.1: Managed sanitation services
  • 6.3.1: Wastewater treatment
  • 6.4.1: Water-use efficiency
  • 6.5.1: Integrated water management
  • 6.6.1: Ecosystem changes over time

SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.

  • 7.1.1: Access to electricity
  • 7.2.1: Renewable energy share
  • 7.3.1: Energy efficiency improvements

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

  • 8.1.1: GDP growth per capita
  • 8.3.1: Informal employment
  • 8.5.1: Unemployment rates
  • 8.7.1: Child labor
  • 8.8.1: Safe work environments

SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

  • 11.1.1: Access to adequate housing
  • 11.2.1: Sustainable transport systems
  • 11.3.1: Urban land use
  • 11.6.1: Environmental impact of cities
  • 11.7.1: Access to green spaces

SDG 13: Climate Action

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

  • 13.1.1: Resilience to climate hazards
  • 13.2.1: Integration of climate measures into policies
  • 13.3.1: Climate education and awareness
